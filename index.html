PTE FIB-RW Practice

Business has to satisfy people's needs and wants by producing goods and services. Essential goods and services required for human survival, food, water, shelter, etc. Goods and services which people would like to have but which is not essential for living, examples luxury house, new PC, car and watching movies, etc. , people's wants are never satisfied; they are infinite/unlimited. The resources available to businesses are finite. There are not enough factors of production to make all of the goods and services satisfy people's needs and unlimited wants.
